    Mobile-web design for virtual account

    OVERVIEW


    Virtual accounts, also known as virtual accounts or
    virtual IBANs, are digital representation of traditional
    bank accounts that allow individuals and businesses
    to receive and make payments electronically.

    ROLE

    Product Designer
    Research, interaction, Visual design,
    Prototyping & Testing

    GOAL

    The goal of this particular project was to identify the 
challenges people, business owners, individual go 
through during their daily business transactions. 
And also to give a seamless solution to this 
challenges faced by these people.

    Introduction

    The banking industry has undergone massive technological transformations in the past few decades,
    and one of the most significant advancements is the introduction of virtual accounts. Virtual
    accounts are digital representations of traditional bank accounts that exit only in the digital world.
    in this case study. I examine the benefits and limitations of virtual accounts, how businesses use
    them, and also the design approach created by me for Bani Africa's client base.

    Background

    Bani Africa is a Fintech company that provide payment services such as virtual accounts, payment
    link and more. They are building alternative payment infrastructure for the Global Economy. Which
    means they are building infrastructure that is super easy for any business to easily and seamlessly
    collect alternative payments from their customers.

    Process



    My design process required three phases:

    1. Brainstorming together with the other designer and the stakeholders

    2. Prototyping different solutions

    3. Designing the UI screens with the interaction

    Outcome



    The few mockups below show the empty state of a merchant dashboard for virtual account, the
    dashboard when active, and the merchant branch activity page.
